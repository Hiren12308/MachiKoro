How to Play

Goal: First player to build all 8 landmarks wins.

Turn: Roll dice → Income → Buy 1 thing (mandatory if affordable) → End turn.

One purchase per turn — card or landmark, not both.

■ Blue — activates on anyone's roll, collect from bank.

■ Green — activates on your roll only, collect from bank.

■ Red — activates on active player's roll, they pay you.

■ Purple — activates on your roll, unique effects. Max 1 per player.

High Roll Row (7+) unlocks once any player builds Train Station (allows 2 dice).

Stacked cards mean duplicates came up — the ×N badge shows how many copies are available.
